# Cookies & Analytics

NOMANA-IT respects your privacy. This page explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on `docs.nomana-it.fr` and how to control them.

## What we use

| Type | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Analytics cookies (`_ga`, `_ga_*`) | Google Analytics 4 | Aggregated audience measurement: page views, sessions, country / region. | 13 months max |
| Local storage (`nomana-cookie-consent`) | NOMANA-IT | Remembers your consent choice. | 13 months |

No cookie is used for advertising, profiling or cross-site tracking.

## Legal basis

Analytics cookies are deposited **only with your prior consent**, in line with [Article 82 of the French Data Protection Act](https://www.cnil.fr/fr/cookies-et-autres-traceurs/regles/cookies-solutions-pour-les-outils-de-mesure-daudience) and the CNIL's guidance on audience measurement. **No analytics cookie is placed before you click *Accept*** in the banner.

## What Google receives

When you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics 4 receives:

- the page URL, referrer, and visit timestamp;
- a pseudonymous identifier (the `_ga` cookie value);
- your IP address, **anonymized** (the last octet is truncated) before storage;
- approximate location (country / region) derived from the IP;
- device information (browser, operating system, screen size).

Google processes this data on our behalf as a *processor* under the [Google Analytics Data Processing Terms](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/3379636). Data is retained for 14 months in the GA4 property.

## Your choices

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Accept** in the banner | Loads Google Analytics. Saves your choice for 13 months. |
| **Refuse** in the banner | No analytics is loaded. Saves your choice for 13 months. |
| **🍪 Cookie preferences** (bottom-left, on every page) | Re-opens the banner — change your mind at any time. |
| Browser private / incognito window | All storage clears at the end of the session — the banner reappears next time. |

You can also block GA4 with browser features (Firefox Enhanced Tracking Protection, Brave Shields, Safari ITP) or extensions (uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, Ghostery).
